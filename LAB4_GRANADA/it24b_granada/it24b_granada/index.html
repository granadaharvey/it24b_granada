Biography

Name: Monkey D. Luffy

Age: 17 years old

Gender: Male
Height: 174 cm (5'9")
Weight: 72 kg (158 lbs)
Goal:
Luffy's ultimate goal is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure known as One Piece. This title is more than just a name; it represents the pinnacle of freedom and adventure. For Luffy, becoming the Pirate King is about achieving the highest level of liberty on the seas and living a life unbound by the constraints of society
